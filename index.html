What's inside

Everything between the first message and the signed tenancy.

One tidy place for the listings, tenants, and viewings you currently juggle across chats, notes, and your memory.

WhatsApp import

Paste a chat and Renvo AI pulls out the listing details — price, area, beds, furnishing — into a clean card.

Auto Lead matching

Add an owner listing and Renvo instantly surfaces the tenants who fit — budget, area, rooms — so the right match finds you first.

Deal pipeline

See every active deal in one Kanban board — matched, viewing scheduled, follow-up needed, agreement pending — so nothing falls through the cracks.

Auto reminders

Renvo tracks every tenancy expiry and sends you a reminder at 90, 60, 30, and 14 days out — so you never miss a renewal or lose a commission.

Tenancy agreement generation

Fill in the key details and Renvo generates a ready-to-sign tenancy agreement PDF — send it to owner and tenant directly, no Word doc required.

Instant listing share

Turn any owner listing into a shareable PDF card and any lead profile into a polished image — ready to forward to WhatsApp in seconds.
